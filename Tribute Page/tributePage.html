Frank Lampard

Chelsea FC Legend "Super Frank"

Frank Lampard (left) with former teammate and Chelsea FC captian John Terry.

Quick Stats

  • Frank Lampard was born on June 20th, 1978
  • Frank Lampard made his professional debut on January 31st, 1996 as a sub for West Ham United
  • On August 17th, 1996, Frank Lampard made his first start for West Ham United
  • He scored his first professional hat trick on November 19th, 1997 against Walsall in a League Cup match
  • Earned his first England cap on Octover 10th, 1999
  • Tranferred to Chelsea FC in June, 2001
  • Named to the 2004 PFA Team of the Year
  • Helped win Chelsea FC's first title in 50 years in 2005
  • Frank Lampard is Chelsea FC's all-time leading goalscorer
  • He has won the FA Premier League three times (2004–05, 2005–06, 2009–10)
  • He has won the FA Cup four times (2006–07, 2008–09, 2009–10, 2011–12)
  • He won the UEFA Champions League in 2011-12
  • Frank Lampard transferred to New York City FC in 2014
  • He retired after the 2015-16 season
"He was a great professional, and a great example to follow. Arriving in England, I remember Didier Drogba telling me, ‘Just watch (Lampard), because you have to learn.’ (He had a) hunger to score goals and to make the difference, and I’ve seen him score the winning goal so many times."

-Former Chelsea FC teammate Florent Malouda-
